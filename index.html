Campus Life

Established in 1886

Established in 1886, Springlands School is located on the western side of Blenheim, in sunny Marlborough. We offer exciting high quality authentic learning experiences for around 450, Year 0-6 students. Springlands Primary School is a part of a supportive and diverse community. We have always been held in very high regard as a successful, innovative school and strive to make a difference by developing lifelong learners.
